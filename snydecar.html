I present the Snydecar:

  • 2 ounces Bombay Dry Gin
  • 1 ounce Stolichnaya Vodka
  • ½ ounce tonic water
  • ½ ounce soda water
  • ¼ ounce Lillet Blanc
  • ¼ ounce dry vermouth
  • Shake over ice until chilled but not ice cold
  • Serve with star anise or a single olive in a non-stemmed martini glass
