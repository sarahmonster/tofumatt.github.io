Open Source

I like to work on open source whenever I can. I'm familiar with all sorts of languages, but I'm often found coding in C/C++, JavaScript, PHP, and Ruby.

One of my bigger open-source projects is Prey Fetcher, which provides free Twitter notifications for iOS devices. It was launched in the summer of 2009 and has delivered over half a million notifications.

I also wrote Skarmory, a Twitter library for Appcelerator Titanium. It allows iOS and Android developers using Appcelerator to easily integrate Twitter into their existing iOS app or leverage the library to write a Twitter app.
